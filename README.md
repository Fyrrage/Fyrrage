Hytale 2026 #1 ModJam Entry

Theme: 
  Echoes of the Machine

Mod Name:
 	Geared Centurion Armor Set

  
Small Description:
 	Stamina-enhancing armor, inspired by fantasy machinery and energy.

Description:
 
  Stamina Enhancement:
    
  Geared Centurion Armor & its rare counter-part --- Energized Centurion Armor both provides extra stamina with each piece, on top of protection between Iron-Tier and Thorium-Tier.

  Two Variants:
    
  Geared Centurion Armor has opened eye sockets, allowing players to see facial expression underneath the helmet, While Energized Centurion Armor has glowing effect on every pieces.

  Both armor sets can be looted from ruined keeps in Zone 4.

  Idea Background (Lore):
    
  Legends tells that the greatest inventors of Crucifix has found a magical energy during their research. 

  The joy of discovery however, was soon devoured by a lust for domination. What better way to show-off this extra-ordinary power, than an army of man... armed with inhuman strength and incredible prowess in battle?

  Needless to say this singular act of tyrannism has resulted in their eventual downfall, these scars of catastrophe are still stored deep within the ruins of their deceased creator.
